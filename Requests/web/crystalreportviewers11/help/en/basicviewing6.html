Step 2: Select a condition for your results

To use the Expert mode
  1. Click the Conditions tab and select a field and filter from the drop-down lists.
  2. Enter a value to search for.

    3. Note:    You cannot use wildcard characters, such as * or ?, in the Value field.

  3. If you want to add more than one filter to your condition, click Add more filters from beneath the Field drop-down list.

    4. If you add more filters than you are using, you can leave the unused filters blank, and they will be ignored when you view the results.

  4. When you are finished adding conditions, go to Step 3: Results.

Tip:    If you are using the Free Form mode and you want to switch to the Expert mode, click the Expert button below the text box. If your condition is complicated or contains complex filters, you will not be able to switch to the Expert mode.

To use the Free Form mode
  1. Click the Conditions tab and type a condition in the text box.

    2. Use the items listed in the drop-down list to add report fields to your condition.

    Note:    

    • Values you want to search for must be enclosed in quotation marks in your condition. For example: {Product_Type.Product Type Name} = "Helmets".
    • You cannot use wildcard characters, such as * or ?, in your condition's definition.
    • For more information about how to enter conditions in the Expert mode, see the Crystal Reports Online Help (Crw.chm) and search for the topic "Specifying formulas."
  2. Go to Step 3: Results.

Tip:    If you are using the Expert mode and you want to switch to the Free Form mode, click the Free Form button below the Value text box.
