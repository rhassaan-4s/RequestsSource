Using the Advanced Search Wizard

 When you click the Show/Hide Advanced Search Wizard button, the wizard appears at the top of the viewer. The wizard lets you perform a search on your report data using Boolean operators. Depending on how your Administrator set up your report, the wizard might start on any of its three tabs.

Note:    To close the Advanced Search Wizard, click the Show/Hide Advanced Search Wizard button again.

In some cases, you may not have the Advanced Search Wizard available in your viewer. This depends on how your Administrator has set up the report.

Click the appropriate link to jump to that section:

  • Step 1: Select the fields from the report to display
  • Step 2: Select a condition for your results
  • Step 3: Results

Note:    In order to save your changes in a step, you must complete that step before moving on to the next one.
